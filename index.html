About us

Alan Gómez Pasillas is the real CEO of the enterprise, he does all the work that the company makes at least for now, he loves drawing, programming, music, innovation, creativity and nature. He wants to be a good person and wants everyone to be happy. Alan has born in Mexico, it was august 16th 2001, he used to play videogames with his father since he was a kid, that is possibly the reason he loves making videogames.

Mystic Prisma is just a small enterprise that wants to share art and good things to the world. Nowadays there's only one worker here, the CEO, we are planning to grow the company when we are able to do it and provide jobs to animators, graphic designers and programmers that are entry level, so they can have money from what they like and also learn about working for a company that has those needs.

We have made a little bunch of videogames already, Mystic Prisma was founded on 2012 by Alan, initially we were known as Alan Games Production Entertainment, and previously had a club called Super Club Chido, after some years AGPE became Mystic Prisma to be open for more future workers and not just Alan.

Some of our most interesting games are TOPEA and Alan el regreso, TOPEA is also a sport created by Alan and developed for android by himself, Alan el regreso is still not finished but it is a platformer that involves fights and a very interesting story.

Amigo Programador is one of our most liked projects, its purpose is to provide quality learning to people that is studying programming and for things that are not very usual right now like assembly, a lot of knowledege is disappearing, and sometimes it is possible to find it but it is still difficult and sometimes to understand as well, that is the main purpose, share valuable knowledge with the world.
